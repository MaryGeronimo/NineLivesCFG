Here at Nine Lives, we strive to know all there is about cats in the media. Of course we are all familiar with The Cat in the Hat, Garfield, Hello Kitty and Grumpy Cat. But what about all the cat game characters out there?? Rover? King Tom? Palicoes? Meowth? Hopefully you recognise that last name! Cats have often been depicted in games as loveable, feisty, sometimes intimidating characters and game developers have cottoned on to just how much the public love those crazy furballs. Over the last few years, adventure games have enabled a petting function when you see a passing cat. The tamagotchi sadly lost its relevance but cat pet simulator games were born. Game developers have now created the ability to play as a cat.

Since the dawn of cinema, in 1895, cats have famously been depicted in live action and animated meowvies. You may be familiar with the work of Garfield and TopCat but we endeavour to reveal some hidden purrformers to you. Here, you will learn more about ‘the cat in Aliens’ and the black cat in Sabrina the Teenage Witch so aptly named Salem. We have even delved into the YouTube sensation that is Owl Kitty, a creator who uses editing software to seamlessly incorporate his cat into classic movie scenes. If you are a film buff, you will love our Furry Flix!
